Dangers and Effects of Commonly Used Drugs

Khat

Khat contains cathinone, an amphetamine-like stimulant. This substance can lead to increased alertness, energy, and talkativeness, but also comes with risks like addiction, cardiovascular problems, psychosis, and mental health issues.

Marijuana

Marijuana contains THC, the main psychoactive compound responsible for relaxation, euphoria, and altered perception. However, regular use can lead to addiction, respiratory problems, impaired cognitive function, and mental health issues.

Here are some additional resources on marijuana:

  • More research

Alcohol

Alcohol is a depressant that slows down the central nervous system. While moderate consumption may be considered safe for some, excessive drinking can lead to addiction, liver damage, heart disease, cancer, and accidents.
