Commonly Abused Drugs

Alcohol

Effects: Impairs judgment, coordination, and memory. Long-term use can lead to addiction and health issues like liver disease.

Statistics: One of the most widely used addictive substances globally.

Marijuana

Effects: Alters mood, can impair memory and cognitive abilities. May also have medicinal uses.

Statistics: One of the most commonly used illicit drugs in the world.

Cocaine

Effects: Produces short-term euphoria, increased energy, and mental alertness. Can cause heart problems and lead to addiction.

Statistics: Highly addictive stimulant drug.

Khat

Effects: Produces a surge of euphoria and relaxation. Highly addictive, leading to severe health issues and overdose risks.

Statistics: Highly addictive opioid drug.
